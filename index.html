About Us

Your Beauty is Our Passion

Edvine Beautys Home Unisex is a premier beauty establishment located in the heart of Achimota Market, Ghana. Since our inception, we have been dedicated to providing high-quality beauty services to enhance the natural beauty of our clients.

Our team of skilled professionals specializes in makeup artistry, nail care, lash services, facial treatments, and more. We pride ourselves on staying updated with the latest beauty trends and techniques to offer you the best experience.

At Edvine Beautys, we believe that everyone deserves to feel beautiful and confident. That's why we offer personalized services that cater to your unique beauty needs and preferences.
